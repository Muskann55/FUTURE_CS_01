\# Phishing Email Analysis



\## Email Classification



PHISHING



\## Indicators Found



1\. Urgent Language

2\. Suspicious Domain

3\. Threat of Account Closure

4\. Credential Harvesting Attempt



\## Risk Level



HIGH



\## Recommendations



\- Verify sender address

\- Check domain carefully

\- Enable MFA

\- Avoid clicking unknown links

\- Report suspicious emails
